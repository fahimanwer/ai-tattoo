## Key Takeaways

### Revenue Leaders
1. **Tattoo AI (HUBX)** — $300K/mo (dominant leader)
2. **InkDNA** — $50K/mo (fast-growing)
3. **InkAI** — $30K/mo (best monetization per download)

### Common Feature Set Across Top Apps
- AI text-to-tattoo generation
- AR / virtual try-on
- Multiple tattoo styles (traditional, Japanese, tribal, minimalist, etc.)
- Custom tattoo fonts
- Save, share, and export designs
- Stencil creation

### Pricing Patterns
- **Weekly:** $6.99 - $9.99 (most common entry point)
- **Annual:** $29.99 - $59.99
- **Lifetime:** $29.99 - $299.99 (wide range)
- Free trial with paywall is the dominant monetization strategy

### Gaps & Opportunities
- Most apps are English-only — multilingual support is a differentiator
- InkAI proves strong monetization is possible without massive downloads
- AR try-on is becoming table stakes — every top app has it
- No app heavily focuses on tattoo artist collaboration/sharing workflow
- Content ratings vary (4+ vs 18+) — lower ratings may help discoverability
- Social sharing features are underutilized by most competitors
